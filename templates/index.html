🌊 What is Ukiyo QR?

Our tool is designed for students, creators, small business owners, and anyone who needs quick QR code generation.

🎌 The Meaning Behind "Ukiyo"

"Ukiyo" (浮世) is a beautiful Japanese word meaning "floating world" or "living in the moment." It represents the philosophy of finding beauty in the fleeting nature of life. This concept inspired the famous ukiyo-e art movement, which gave us masterpieces like Hokusai's "The Great Wave off Kanagawa" — the very artwork that serves as our app's background and favicon.
